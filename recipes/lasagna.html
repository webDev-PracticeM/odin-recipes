Lasagna

Meat lasagna

This is the famous meat lasagna is for the meat lovers!

Make this at home and your family and friends will want seconds or even thirds!

Ingredients

  • Lean ground beef and sweet Italian sausage
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Tomato products
    • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
    • 2 cans of tomato sauce
    • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • Seasoning
    • Fresh parsley
    • Dried basil leaves
    • Salt
    • Italian seasoning
    • Fennel seeds
    • Black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
    • Parmesan
    • Mozzarella
    • Ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
